  • TLX Discord Bot

    TLX Discord Bot is a bot written in Discord.py alongside Selenium for Web-Scraping. The purpose of this bot was to assist our school's Programming Club members to easily access profiles on the Programming Learning Platform. In this bot you can view the Rating, Total AC Problems and Scores of the specified profile. To spice things up, we also added a feature called badges!
